Fashion Group Sales Dashboard

For this project, I created a dynamic Excel dashboard to visualize and analyze the sales data of a major fashion group with two retail chains. Covering the financial years 2015 to 2017, the dataset includes sales information from various US states and product categories. After thoroughly cleaning the data in Excel, I built an interactive dashboard featuring slicers and pivot tables, enabling users to explore sales trends, compare chain performance, and drill down into specific states and categories. This tool provides stakeholders with valuable insights for data-driven decision-making.
